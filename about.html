About SESE

Empowering the next generation of innovators at UC Berkeley

What We Believe

The Berkeley Society of Engineers, Scientists, and Entrepreneurs (SESE) organizes events such as the Career Fair, Research Seminar, and Investment Summit, creating opportunities for students to connect deeply with industry leaders, Nobel laureates, and academic pioneers. SESE's mission is to channel technological talent into both industry and academia while providing engineers with abundant academic resources, career opportunities, and support for advanced development.

Our Core Values

The principles that guide everything we do

Innovation

We encourage creative thinking and embrace new ideas that push the boundaries of what's possible in technology and business.

Collaboration

We believe in the power of teamwork and diverse perspectives to solve complex problems and create meaningful impact.

Learning

We are committed to continuous learning and sharing knowledge to help our members grow both personally and professionally.

Excellence

We strive for excellence in everything we do, from organizing events to supporting our members' professional development.

Impact

We aim to create positive change in our community and beyond through technology, innovation, and social responsibility.

Community

We foster a supportive and inclusive community where everyone feels welcome and can thrive.
